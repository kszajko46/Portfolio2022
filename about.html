"Logic can get you from point A to Point B but imagination can get you anywhere"-Albert Einstein


Please, allow me to introduce myself.

Growing up I always thought of Albert Einstein's quote and used that to keep pushing myself forward. If you can't imagine yourself somewhere, how do you get there? where do you start?
I had so many toughts growing up of who I thought I might want to be, I imagined myself in all of those positions and tried to decide if I would be happy, would I continue to enjoy it. The endless questions but I never found myself happy. I honestly felt lost, I didn't know what to do or where to go but I kept moving forward.
While trying to find the place I felt like I belong I had wonderful, beautiful, and some tragic adventures. I have a beautiful, smart, kind daughter who means the world to me. I have freinds and family that I am so greatful for because without them and their support I don't think my imagination would have ever brought me to this point of my life.
